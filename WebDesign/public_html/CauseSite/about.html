General Knowledge

Cancer is a group of diseases that cause abnormal cell growth. This abnormal growth causes certain areas of the body to expand and take up more resources and energy than they are supposed to. For example, if a tumor was growing in someone's brain, and it's growth put pressure on the area of the brain that creates memories, that person could experience memory loss.

Treatment

The most widespread treament used today is chemotherapy. In chemotherapy, raditation is used to kill the cancer cells. Exposing them to radiation causes the cells to malfunction, and usually die. However, the main risk in this treatment option is that this radiation can kill healthy cells also. This is why many people experince heavy side effects from chemotherapy, like nausia, fatigue, and pain. There are also a bunch of other methods of treatment, including herbal treatments and stress relief tactics, designed to help the person fight of the cancer with the power of their mind and psyche. Although it may seem ridiculous, this style has actually worked on a lot of patients, and is my personal favorite.
